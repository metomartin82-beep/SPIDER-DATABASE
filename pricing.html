Pricing questions

What counts as a "database"?

Each database is a fully isolated instance — typically one per user, tenant, or organization in your app. A database you create and never delete continues to count against your plan's limit even if it's empty.

What happens if I go over my plan's limits?

On Free and Starter, database creation is blocked once you hit your limit until you upgrade or delete unused databases. On Scale and Enterprise, usage above the included amount is billed per unit at the end of the month — nothing is throttled.

Can I pause databases I'm not using?

Yes. Paused databases stop counting toward your active read/write quota but keep their storage and backups intact, so you can resume them instantly later.
